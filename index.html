Primary Typeface
Aa Bb Cc 123 Regular · body copy

Use Bold for headlines, social graphics, and CTAs.
Use Regular for body text, captions, and secondary copy.

Logo

  • Maintain clear space around the logo
  • Use only approved color versions — no recoloring
  • Do not stretch, rotate, or add effects
  • Avoid busy or low-contrast backgrounds

Photography

Do
  • Use high-quality, human-first photography
  • Feature a diverse range of families and newborns
  • Prioritize warmth, authenticity, natural light
  • Use illustrations and icons when relevant and on-brand
Don't
  • Use AI-generated images
  • Use clip art or off-brand illustration styles

Tone of Voice

Baby Connect speaks like a trusted friend who happens to be an expert — calm, caring, and practical. We use plain language, never jargon, and always put the family first.
